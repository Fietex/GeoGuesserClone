How to Play

You will get a random Streetview in a german city. Your task is to guess in which city this Streetview is. Write your Guess in the input at the bottom of the page and press Enter.

You will earned Points for each correct guess. But if your Guess is wrong you loose all.

You can skip a Location by pressing New Location in the top right corner.
